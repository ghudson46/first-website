How to make a sandwhich

  1. Get bread
  2. Get peanut butter and jelly
  3. Spread peanut butter on one piece of bread
  4. Spread jelly on the second piece of bread
  5. Place the two pieces of bread together
  6. Cut diagonally and enjoy!
